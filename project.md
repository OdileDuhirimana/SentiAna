Tech Stack: Python, Transformers (BERT/RoBERTa), LSTM Hybrid Models, Hugging Face, Flask/FastAPI, Redis, Docker, PostgreSQL, WebSockets, React (Dashboard), CI/CD

🌟 Overview

SentimentScope is a next-generation Emotion AI platform that decodes not just sentiment — but the entire emotional spectrum behind human communication.

It performs real-time, multilabel emotion analysis, detects toxic behavior, understands sarcasm, reveals root causes of user dissatisfaction, and even suggests empathetic responses — making it a powerful engine for customer experience, social monitoring, and mental health support.

This is not just an academic NLP model — it’s a fully scalable AI microservice deployed for production.
